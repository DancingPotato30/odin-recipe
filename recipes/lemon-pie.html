Lemon Pie

Description:

Lemon pies are a delicacy that might be too sour for some upon first hearing about it, but it really is not that bad!

They are extremely yummy and topped with merinuge! And today we shall teach you how to make them at the comfort of your own home!

Ingredients:

  • Pie:
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 1/4 cup cornstarch
    • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 2 cups water
    • 3 large egg yolks, beaten, room temperature
    • 1 tablespoon butter
    • 1/4 cup lemon juice
    • 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
    • 1 pastry shell (9 inches), baked
  • Meringue
    • 3 large egg whites, room temperature
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 cup sugar

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine sugar, cornstarch, flour and salt.
  2. Gradually stir in water.
  3. Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened and bubbly.
  4. Reduce heat; cook and stir 2 minutes more.
  5. Remove from the heat.
  6. Gradually stir 1 cup of hot mixture into egg yolks; return all to saucepan.
  7. Bring to boil. Cook and stir for 2 minutes.
  8. Remove from the heat.
  9. Stir in the butter, lemon juice and zest until smooth.
  10. Pour into pastry shell.

  11. In a bowl, beat egg whites and salt until stiff (but not dry) peaks form.
  12. Gradually beat in sugar until soft peaks form.
  13. Spread over pie,sealing edges to pastry.
  14. Bake at 350Â° until meringue is golden, 12-15 minutes.
  15. Cool.
  16. Store any leftovers in the refrigerator.
